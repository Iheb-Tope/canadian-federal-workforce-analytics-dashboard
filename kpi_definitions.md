# KPI Definitions

## Total Employees
Sum of employee counts in the selected reporting context.

## Department Count
Distinct count of departments/agencies in the selected reporting context.

## Top Department by Headcount
Department with the highest employee count in the selected reporting context.

## Employee Change Selected Period
Difference between employee count in the latest selected year and employee count in the earliest selected year.

## Employee Change % Selected Period
(Employee Change Selected Period) / (Employees in Earliest Selected Year)

## NCR Employees
Employee count associated with the National Capital Region category in the work location dataset.

## Outside Canada Employees
Employee count associated with the Outside of Canada category in the work location dataset.

## Workforce Composition by Organization Type
Distribution of employees across organization-type categories available in the overall population dataset.

## Workforce Composition by Tenure Type
Distribution of employees across tenure type categories such as indeterminate, term, casual, student, and unknown.

## Workforce Distribution by Age Band
Distribution of employees across age categories such as 20–24, 25–29, and 65+.

## Geographic Distribution
Employee count by simplified province/territory mapping field used for dashboard geography visuals.
